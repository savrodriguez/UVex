How Does It Work?

Simply apply the transparent patch to your skin, and as you bask in the sun,
the patch subtly transitions to a vibrant purple hue over time. This color change serves as a visual cue,
indicating the need to reapply sunscreen for continued protection against harmful UV rays. Once you've applied sunscreen,
watch as the patch seamlessly returns to its clear state, ready to keep you informed for up to 8 hours.
